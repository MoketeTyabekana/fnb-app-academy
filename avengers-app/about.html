    About Avengers

    The Avengers are a fictional superhero team appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. The team made its debut in "The Avengers" #1, which was released in September 1963. Created by writer Stan Lee and artist Jack Kirby, the Avengers are known for their diverse roster of superheroes who come together to combat powerful threats that no single hero could handle alone.
